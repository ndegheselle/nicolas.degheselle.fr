Dynamic, versatile language essential for web development, known for its ease of use and extensive ecosystem. 

## Feedback

- Everything is easy to do, and so is making bad design choices.
- Sharing common libraries is cumbersome (requires builds, handle import format, easy to overlook). 
- Generic types are complex in advanced use cases (e.g., CRUD for Prisma).
